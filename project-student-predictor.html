Educational institutions struggled to identify at-risk students until grades had already slipped beyond recovery. Manual tracking of 2,000+ students across 15 academic parameters was unsustainable — counselors were overwhelmed, and data lived in disconnected spreadsheets.

The Solution

Prediction at scale.

I built a Random Forest classifier using Python and Scikit-learn, engineered 12 custom features from raw attendance, assignment, and exam data, and trained the model on 3 years of historical records — over 6,000 student entries. The result: an early warning system that flags students weeks before traditional methods.

The Architecture

End-to-end pipeline.

Data flowed from SQL extraction through Pandas cleaning, into custom feature engineering, model training, and finally Power BI visualization. I used 5-fold stratified cross-validation to ensure robust performance across class imbalances, and fine-tuned hyperparameters with GridSearchCV for optimal precision-recall balance.
